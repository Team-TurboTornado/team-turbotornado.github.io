\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages the linking of IMontaviz\+Effects to Game\+Objects. Im a Transform-\/\+Hierarchy an effect of a parent does not affect the effects of a child if the child is also linked by this manager. 

Imagine the following Object Structure\+: $\vert$-\/ A $\vert$-\/B $\vert$-\/C $\vert$-\/D 1) An Effect E1 is applied to A E1 affects A, B, C, D (all Children of A and A itself) 2) Another Effect E2 is applied to B E2 affects B, C (all children of B and B itself) But\+: E1 no longer affects B and C because they are linked to their own effect now

The term \char`\"{}being linked by this manager\char`\"{} refers to a Game\+Object being present in \+\_\+effect\+Data\+By\+Object. Objects are linked through by of \mbox{\hyperlink{class_controls_1_1_highlighting_controls_1_1_effect_manager_1_1_hierarchical_effect_manager_af25c314703fd65182a2d302bcb6c1161}{Activate\+Effect}} (no matter the arguments) and unlinked by calls of \mbox{\hyperlink{class_controls_1_1_highlighting_controls_1_1_effect_manager_1_1_hierarchical_effect_manager_a0e1b820c031c3f61c4ac4d9c164648f2}{Remove\+Effects}}.

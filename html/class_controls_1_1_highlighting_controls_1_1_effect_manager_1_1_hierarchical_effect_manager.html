Detailed Description

Manages the linking of IMontavizEffects to GameObjects. Im a Transform-Hierarchy an effect of a parent does not affect the effects of a child if the child is also linked by this manager.

Imagine the following Object Structure: |- A |-B |-C |-D 1) An Effect E1 is applied to A E1 affects A, B, C, D (all Children of A and A itself) 2) Another Effect E2 is applied to B E2 affects B, C (all children of B and B itself) But: E1 no longer affects B and C because they are linked to their own effect now

The term "being linked by this manager" refers to a GameObject being present in _effectDataByObject. Objects are linked through by of ActivateEffect (no matter the arguments) and unlinked by calls of RemoveEffects.
